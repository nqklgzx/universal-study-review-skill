# Example: AI Math Foundations Review Workflow

## Material inputs

- Course textbook or lecture notes
- Linear algebra, probability, optimization, and calculus chapters
- Homework and exam-style problems
- Teacher review notes

## First prompt

```text
Use $universal-study-review to create a source-grounded review workflow for my AI Math Foundations course. Build a material index, concept map, proof/problem templates, formula sheet, and quick-review notes. Preserve assumptions, theorem conditions, symbols, and derivation steps.
```

## Expected outputs

- `ai_math_material_index.md`
- `ai_math_concept_map.md`
- `ai_math_formula_theorem_sheet.md`
- `ai_math_standard_solution_templates.md`
- `ai_math_quick_review.md`

## Quality checks

- Theorem assumptions are written before conclusions.
- Symbols and dimensions are checked.
- Proof steps are not replaced by vague explanation.
- Similar methods are compared through use conditions.

# Example: Signals and Systems Review Workflow

## Material inputs

- Course slides by chapter
- Textbook excerpts
- Formula notes
- Problem sets and worked solutions
- Teacher lecture notes or recording summaries

## First prompt

```text
Use $universal-study-review to build a source-grounded material index for my Signals and Systems course. Preserve file names, chapter structure, slide/page locations, formulas, transform pairs, problem types, and teacher-priority notes. Do not invent page numbers or formulas.
```

## Expected outputs

- `signals_and_systems_material_index.md`
- `signals_and_systems_module_map.md`
- `signals_and_systems_formula_and_method_sheet.md`
- `signals_and_systems_problem_type_templates.md`
- `signals_and_systems_quick_review.md`

## Quality checks

- Transform formulas include conditions and domains.
- Problem solutions state method choice and derivation steps.
- ROC, convergence, causality, stability, and frequency-domain conditions are not omitted.
- Teacher priorities are separated from inferred priorities.
